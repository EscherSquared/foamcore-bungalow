PROJECT BRIEF

Build a one-storey bungalow from a single sheet of foam core.

You will pick one of the three designs below. Each design is engineered to fit on one standard 50 × 76 cm foam core sheet, including the floor base. Cut lists, dimensions, and exploded views are all provided. Work carefully, measure twice, cut once.

RULES OF THE BENCH

  1. Always cut on an appropriate surface.
  2. Use a sharp blade — change blades when they drag.
  3. Use a straight edge (e.g. ruler) as a cutting guide.
  4. Score first, then cut clean on the second pass.
  5. Hot glue burns. Cool glue holds. Be patient.

Which Design Should You Pick?

CHOOSE A IF…

You want a sharp, contemporary look and the simplest cut list. Best for first-time foam core builders.

CHOOSE B IF…

You're confident with a ruler and want to push yourself. Most pieces, most measuring, biggest payoff.

CHOOSE C IF…

You like classic, symmetrical houses and want angled cuts that look impressive without being hard.
